About me

I'm Justin Clagg, a graduate of the University of Pittsburgh who studied physics and computer science.

Physics quickly introduced me to programming, used as a means to efficiently solve problems without analytic solutions. After spending a summer writing C++ code at NIST, I started looking into programming as a career.

I love taking on complex problems, developing efficient solutions, and creating new things. Programming has been a perfect fit, and I'm working to learn as much as possible.

I'm currently learning front and back end development, with a focus on JavaScript. I've completed the front-end section of Free Code Camp, and am now working with Node.js and Angular.
